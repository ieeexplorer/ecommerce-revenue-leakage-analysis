# Executive Summary

## Objective
Assess revenue leakage, customer retention, product performance, and delivery reliability for an e-commerce business.

## Key Findings
1. Revenue is led by Electronics and Fashion, but return rates create leakage in some categories.
2. A subset of customers drives a large share of lifetime value.
3. Some regions experience higher delay rates, which may affect customer satisfaction and repeat purchase behavior.
4. Discount-heavy categories show weaker net revenue quality than low-discount categories.
5. A small set of customers have become inactive long enough to qualify as churn-risk.

## Recommendations
1. Review return-heavy categories for product-quality or expectation mismatch.
2. Launch targeted reactivation campaigns for churn-risk high-value customers.
3. Improve operational performance in high-delay regions.
4. Monitor discount strategy to balance conversion against margin.
5. Build a recurring monthly KPI dashboard from the cleaned analyst-ready table.

## Deliverables
- SQL KPI pack
- Cleaned analytical dataset
- Customer/product/retention analysis notebook
- Chart notebook
- Dashboard specification
